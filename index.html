1. No Collection of Personal Data

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data. This includes:

  • Names, emails, phone numbers, or addresses.
  • Location data (except for Google Maps functionality, which we do not control).
  • Device identifiers (e.g., IP addresses, IMEI numbers).

You can use the app fully without providing any personal information.

2. Cache Storage

The app stores non-personal data in your device’s cache to:

  • Save your progress within the app (e.g., completed lessons or settings).
  • Improve app performance and user experience.

This data:

  • Is stored only on your device and not shared with anyone.
  • Is temporary and may be deleted automatically by your device.
  • Can be erased by uninstalling the app or clearing your device’s cache.

3. Internet Access & Google Maps

The app requires internet access to display Google Maps for specific locations. This functionality:

  • Is provided by Google Maps, and we do not control or access any data processed by Google.
  • May involve Google collecting data as described in their Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Maps to display maps. We have no control over how Google handles your data. Refer to Google’s policies for details.

5. Permissions

The app does not request any permissions beyond standard internet access required for Google Maps.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here, and the "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect the revision.
